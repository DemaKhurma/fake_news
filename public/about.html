Project Idea

Our project, Fake News Detector, is an intelligent AI system that helps users verify the credibility of news circulating on social media.

Why We Made This

In today's world, most people use social media to receive news. Millions of users share news daily without verifying its accuracy. This can cause serious problems: misleading public opinion, spreading rumors, and negative effects on mental, political, and social well-being.

Goal

To provide a simple and user-friendly tool. Users just copy and paste the news or its link into the site and press the analyze button to see if it is real or fake.
